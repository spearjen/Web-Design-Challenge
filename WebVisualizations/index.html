What's the perfect latitude for your attitude?


The purpose of this project is to analyze how weather changes in relationship to distance from the equator. Five hundred cities were queried using OpenWeatherMapAPI.

Utilizing Matplotlib to generate visuals, plots show the relationship of latitude to temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speeds.

Source data is provided on the Data page. Visualizations may be found under the Plots dropdown, with an overview at the Comparisons page.
