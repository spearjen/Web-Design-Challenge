Comparisons: Latitude vs. X

The following scatterplots compare specific weather datapoints across latitude.

Latitude vs. Max Temperature

As you move further away from the equator in either Northern
or Southern Hemisphere, max temperatures drop

Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Latitude has little effect on Cloudiness in either Northern
or Southern Hemisphere.

Latitude vs. Humidity

Humidity is higher at the equator, dropping between 20 and 40
degrees latitude away from the equator, and then rising again.

Latitude vs. Wind Speeds

Latitude has little effect on wind speeds.
